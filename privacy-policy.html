Privacy Policy

App: Royal Blocks · Last updated: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Royal Blocks ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use the App. By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not ask you to create an account and we do not collect your name, email, or contact details directly within the App. However, the following information may be collected automatically:

  • Advertising & device identifiers (such as the Apple Advertising Identifier / IDFA) collected by our advertising partner to show and measure ads.
  • Usage & diagnostic data (e.g., interactions with ads, app performance, crash data) used to operate and improve advertising and the App.
  • Purchase information for in-app purchases, processed by Apple. We receive only confirmation of a completed purchase, not your payment details.
  • Locally stored game data (coins, level progress, high score, settings) is saved on your device only and is not transmitted to us.

2. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On first launch the App asks for your permission to track using Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized. You can change this any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may process data under their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store / In-App Purchase — https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

4. How We Use Information

To display and measure advertisements, process in-app purchases, maintain and improve the App, and comply with legal obligations.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

6. Data Retention

Game progress is stored locally on your device and is removed if you delete the App. Data processed by third parties is retained according to their respective policies.

7. Your Choices

You may limit ad tracking via your device settings, decline the tracking prompt, or delete the App to remove locally stored data.
